Smart Chicken House:
Microcontroller is 3.3V
Transistor BC548C uses 0.7V
FAN Specs:
- 12 Volt
- 0.23 Ampere

IBE = IC/HFE = 0.23/800 = 0.0002875 IBE
R = U/IBE = 3.3V-0.7V/0.0002875 = 9043 --> 10k Ohm



Smart Bedroom:

Transistor BC548C uses 0.7V
FAN Specs:
- 12 Volt
- 0.11 Ampere

IBE = IC/HFE = 0.11/800 = 0.0001375 IBE
R = U/IBE = 3.3V-0.7V/0.0001375 = 18909 --> 20k Ohm but I used 22k
